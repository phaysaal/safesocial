Spheres is a decentralized social network designed for close-knit circles. No algorithms, no ads, and zero server-side storage. Just you and the people who matter most.

Download Spheres v0.1.0

Built for your Close Circle

Most social networks want your time. We want your trust. Spheres is built to replace the "noise" with a high-fidelity connection to your real world.

Anti-Addiction

By removing algorithmic feeds and "discovery" tabs, we eliminate the endless scroll. See what your friends are doing, then get back to your life.

No Network Pollution

Spheres is a utility, not a broadcast platform. It is designed for verified contacts only, preventing spam, bots, and public harassment.

Zero Server Metadata

Traditional apps know who you talk to even if they can't read your messages. Spheres hides your social graph from everyone, including us.

"Military Grade" Security

Technically honest privacy. We've moved the goalposts for what a mobile app can protect.

🛡️

Double Ratchet Forward Secrecy

Every single message uses a unique, ephemeral key. If your device is compromised today, your message history remains encrypted and unreadable.

🧅

Onion Routing (Veilid)

All traffic is routed through multiple peer nodes. Your IP address is never revealed to your contacts or the network infrastructure.

👥

Social Identity Recovery

No phone number or email required. Trust your close circle with encrypted fragments of your key via Shamir's Secret Sharing to rebuild your identity if you lose your phone.

✨

Metadata Scrubbing

Our app automatically strips GPS coordinates, camera models, and timestamps from photos and videos before they are shared, preventing accidental location leaks.

⚡

Zero-Knowledge Signaling

Our relay uses salted SHA-256 room identifiers. Even with physical access to our relay, an observer cannot tell who is talking to whom.

🦀

Auditable Rust Core

All security logic is implemented in memory-safe Rust, protecting the app from the buffer overflow exploits typically used in targeted surveillance.
