"Military Grade" Security

Spheres v0.3.9 features our most advanced privacy architecture yet.

🛡️

Double Ratchet Forward Secrecy

Every single message uses a unique, ephemeral key. Past message history remains unreadable even if current keys are compromised.

🧅

Onion Routing (Veilid)

All traffic is masked by multiple peer nodes. Your IP address is never revealed to your contacts or the network infrastructure.

👥

Social Identity Recovery

No phone number or email required. Trust your close circle with encrypted fragments of your key via Shamir's Secret Sharing.

📦

Encrypted Vault Backups

Export your entire social network and history into a single, passphrase-secured .spheres file for safe cold storage.

💍

Audience Rings

Organize contacts into private circles. You decide exactly which group sees every post through multi-wrapped encryption.

✨

Metadata Scrubbing

The app automatically strips GPS coordinates and device IDs from media before sharing, preventing accidental location leaks.
